Delete Your Account

OnTheWay by Aaron Culp

We're sorry to see you go. If you'd like to delete your OnTheWay account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to support@otwapp.com from the email address associated with your OnTheWay account.
  2. Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Include your username or display name in the email body so we can locate your account.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been deleted.

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.

What Data Is Deleted

Permanently deleted upon request:

  • Account credentials (email, password)
  • Profile information (username, display name, bio, profile photo)
  • Demographic data (age range, gender, interests)
  • Check-in history
  • Reviews and ratings you submitted
  • Friend connections and shared collections
  • Flash offer claim history
  • Notification preferences and device tokens
  • Subscription and billing records

What Data May Be Retained

Retained for up to 90 days after deletion:

  • Anonymized, aggregated analytics data (e.g., venue visit counts) that cannot be linked back to your identity
  • Records required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution

After the retention period, all remaining data is permanently purged from our systems.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the deletion process, please contact us at support@otwapp.com.
